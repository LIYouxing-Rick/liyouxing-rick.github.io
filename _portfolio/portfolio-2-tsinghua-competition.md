## Overview

Participated in the **13th Tsinghua Economic Hotspot Analysis Competition** (July – October 2023), a nationally recognized competition organized by Tsinghua University. Our team achieved a **Top 1%** ranking among all participating teams nationwide.

## Research Topic

**AI-Enabled Coal Mining Transformation**: Addressing labor shortages and safety issues in the coal mining industry through artificial intelligence.

## Methodology & Contributions

### Quantitative Modeling
- Built a **Cobb-Douglas production function model** analyzing data from 2000 to 2022
- Revealed a **23.6% increase** in technology contribution rate to coal mining productivity
- Provided empirical evidence for the economic value of AI adoption in traditional industries

### Field Research
- Conducted on-site research at **6 coal mining enterprises**
- Mapped AI applications across **12 operational scenarios**
- Gathered first-hand data on implementation challenges and opportunities

### Policy Framework
- Proposed a **"Four-in-One" transformation framework** combining:
  - Technical architecture for AI deployment
  - Workforce reskilling programs
  - Safety management systems
  - Policy recommendations for industry stakeholders
